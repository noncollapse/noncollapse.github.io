👤 About Me

  1. I am a PhD student in the Department of Statistics at The London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE), advised by Chengchun Shi.
  2. Prior to i, I completed an MSc in Applicable Mathematics at LSE.
  3. Before ii, I received a BSc in Mathematics with Finance from The University of Liverpool and Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University.

🔬 Research

My current research focuses on the intersection of reinforcement learning and large language models, with particular interest in:

  • Offline reinforcement learning and policy optimization
  • Confounded and partially observable environments (POMDPs)
  • Human feedback alignment and preference-based learning
  • Double robust estimation and variance-reduction techniques
  • High-dimensional statistics and distributional analysis
