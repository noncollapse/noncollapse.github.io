My current research focuses on the intersection of reinforcement learning and large language models, with particular interest in:

  • Offline reinforcement learning and policy optimization
  • Confounded and partially observable environments (POMDPs)
  • Human feedback alignment and preference-based learning

  • High-dimensional statistics and distributional analysis
